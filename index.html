A new leash on life. Training friends for life one step at a time

The goal of "A New Leash on Life" is to help you teach your pet desired behaviors while at the same time, keeping that unique spark that makes your dog special to you. The results can be one of the most rewarding relationships of your life.

One of the top reasons dogs are turned in to shelters is because they are not obedient. We will provide you with the necessary tools and instruction to effectively train your dog. There are many factors that shape a dog's behavior besides training including nutrition, environment and socialization. With positive and effective training methods, your dog can become an intelligent and happy member of the family.

Obedience Classes

Private Lessons

Lessons are conducted in your home and are an hour long. The act of you working with your dog as a team goes a long way in forging a lasting bond. It provides a critical foundation in which to correct and even prevent certain behavioral problems.

(5 Months and Up) The basic obedience course is a seven week program with classes being an hour long. Emphasis of this course includes basic obedience commands, behavior problems, nutrition and education. We believe that whoever said "You Can’t Teach An Old Dog New Tricks", just didn't know how.
